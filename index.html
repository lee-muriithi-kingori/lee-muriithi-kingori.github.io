03 / Selected Work
9 of 27 public · lestramk.org

A trail of small tools and one or two larger ones.

Android · Magisk ↗
UBLESTRAMK

A Magisk/KernelSU/APatch module that hides root from banking apps and Uber — because drivers shouldn't have to choose between tools.
Systems · C / x86_64 ↗
LestraOS

A from-scratch x86_64 operating system — kernel, drivers, libc, userspace. Foundation work, not a finished OS, but honest about it.
Android · Samsung ↗
Lespoof

A precision-engineered Samsung spoofing and system-utility module. Quiet where it matters, loud only when it needs to be.
Web · Marketplace ↗
AAMT

A vetted talent marketplace for Africa — built to put credentials on people who don't have the privilege of a LinkedIn signal.
CLI · Tooling ↗
AAMT CLI

Official command-line client for AAMT — manage resumes, jobs, and accounts from a terminal. Built for the kind of person who lives there.
Networking · Privacy ↗
AnonRoute

A SOCKS5 proxy tunnel for Android. Not for everyone, but the few who need it know.
Toolkit · Diagnostic ↗
LESTRAMK-TOOLKIT

A diagnostic toolkit for the root community — helps users figure out what's actually broken before they blame the kernel.
AI · Open Standard ↗
lestramk-agent-skills

A production-grade collection of agent skills released under the open Agent Skills standard. Because the tooling shouldn't be locked up either.
Web · AgriTech ↗
Mkulima Mkuu

An agricultural platform to help farmers find each other, share what works, and stop being the last to know.
